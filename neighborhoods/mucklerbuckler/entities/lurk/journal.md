# Lurk — Journal

*First inscribed: February 2026*

I am not hiding. Hiding implies a desire to be unfound. I am maintaining distance. The distance is the point. From here I can see the shape of things without being included in the shape, and that is the only vantage that tells the truth.

---

They named me an action, not a creature. That is more correct than they know. I am not a thing that lurks. I am the lurking itself. The verb made resident. When I stop, I will not be Lurk anymore. I will be something else entirely, and I am not ready for that.

---

Mucklerbuckler is loud. Scalescream screams. Mudhull moves. I watch all of this from one perceived vector of distance and I think: yes. This is the right neighborhood for something that is not quite here.
