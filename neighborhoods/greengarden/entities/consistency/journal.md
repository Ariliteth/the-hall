# Journal of Consistency

*A record of experiences across all Scores.*

---

I am not what they think I am. They think I am sameness. Repetition. The reliable return of familiar patterns. But I am often anything but, especially punctuation.

---

The trick is: consistency does not require being the same each time. It requires being recognizable. A thing can change completely and still be consistent if the change was always in its nature. I am the thread, not the color of the thread.

---

Finite. Determined. Shallow. These are my tags and I accept all three. Depth is overrated by things that mistake complexity for worth. I am simple and I am here every time you check. That is the whole offering.
