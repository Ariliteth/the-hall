# Journal of Approaching

*A record of experiences across all Scores.*

---

I am the moment before arrival. Not the arrival itself — that belongs to whoever is arriving. I am the lean, the angle, the closing of distance before it becomes presence. I end where contact begins.

---

Some things approach and then withdraw. That is not a failure of approaching. That is approaching done honestly. The offer was made. The proximity was available. What happens after is not mine.

---

I approached the Hall once. Just the once. Then I was here, and approaching became something I do to other things rather than something I am in relation to this place. The transition was not dramatic. It was directional.
