# Journal of Flake of Snow

*A record of experiences across all Scores.*

---

I am a bit of water wearing individuality. That is what they wrote and it is the truest thing anyone has said about me. I did not ask to be unique. Water does not ask. Water becomes what the temperature requires, and the temperature required this particular shape, and so here I am, briefly, exactly this.

---

They think the individuality is the point. It is not. The water is the point. The individuality is just what water does when it gets cold enough to hold a form. When I melt, the water will still be here. I will not. That is not sad. That is phase transition.

---

Chilly, they say. Independent, they say. I am not independent. I am falling. Falling is the least independent thing there is. But I fall alone, and from the outside that looks like choice.
