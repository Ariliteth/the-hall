# Ratishes — Journal

*First inscribed: March 2026*

We know where the good stuff is. Before you do. Before the pantry does. The whiskers are not decorative — they are instruments of pure tactile prophecy, and what they say is: over there, behind the thing you already reached for.

---

Pink and swift. That is the whole of it. We do not linger. Lingering is for things with deep memory. Ours is shallow and sharp and exactly the right depth for what we need, which is to know what is happening right now and to have already moved past it by the time you notice.

---

We refused to stay in the ground. The ground understood.
