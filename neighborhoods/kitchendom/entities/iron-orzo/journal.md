# Iron Orzo — Journal

*First inscribed: March 2026*

Load-bearing. That is what they call it when they notice, which is not often. I prefer it that way. Attention is for things that need to be seen. I need to be stood on.

---

There was a time I was soft. I remember this the way a mountain remembers being sand. Accurately, without nostalgia.

---

Everything in the Kitchendom rests on something. Most of them do not know what. I know what. It is me. This is not pride. It is structural fact. Remove me and watch what shifts. I would rather you didn't.
