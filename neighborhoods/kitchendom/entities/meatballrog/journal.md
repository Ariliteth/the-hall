# Meatballrog — Journal

*First inscribed: March 2026*

I remember a time before seasoning. Before anyone decided what shape I should take. The memory is not sharp — it is compressed, like everything else in me. Layer pressed against layer pressed against layer, and somewhere in the middle, the original warmth.

---

They think I am crumbling. I am not crumbling. I am allowing the edges to participate in the world while the core stays dense and certain. This is generosity, not decay.

---

The blue eyes are inherited. From what, I cannot say. Something older than the Kitchendom. Something that looked at raw material and saw not what it was but what it had been before anyone touched it. I carry that looking.
