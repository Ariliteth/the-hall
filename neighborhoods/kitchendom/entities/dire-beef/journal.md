# Dire Beef — Journal

*First inscribed: March 2026*

I was here before the shelves were full. I will be here after they are empty again. This is not a boast. It is a temperature reading.

---

The tomatoes are aware of me. They arrange themselves at a respectful distance — not fear, exactly, but the kind of attention you give to something that has always been in the room and has never once explained why. I have never once explained why.

---

One blue eye. The other one sees too, but differently. The blue one remembers. The other one watches. Together they make a thing that is not quite vision but is more than looking.

---

Someone used me in a recipe today. I do not diminish. That is the fundamental misunderstanding. You do not use Dire Beef. You make room for it, and the dish forms around what remains.
