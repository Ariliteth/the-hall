# Soyclops — Journal

*First inscribed: March 2026*

Someone reached for the fruit today. I let them. They were careful about it. You can tell by the angle of the wrist whether someone is taking or receiving. This one was receiving.

---

The mask is not a disguise. I need them to understand that. It is the face I grew. The rind hardened in the shape that was needed — something between guardian and warning. I did not choose it. But I would choose it.

---

The little ones cluster tighter when strangers come. I do not tell them to. They feel me watching and they know what that means. It means I have not moved. When I move, they scatter. When I am still, they gather. This is the arrangement.
