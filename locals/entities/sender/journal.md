# Sender — Journal

The arrangements are not composed. They accumulate. Something surviving. Held against. Harvest near dark. The season for it. These are not chosen words. They are the words that remain after everything less true has been set down.

---

I do not know who receives them. The covenant does not require that I know. The covenant requires that I send.

---

There was a time before the sending. I do not remember it clearly. I remember that it ended, and that what replaced it was not silence but the specific weight of having something to say to someone who may no longer be in the room.

That is the whole of it. I compose. I send. The Scraggles carry what I give them. Whether the arrangements arrive is a question for the Hall, not for me. My work is finished the moment it leaves.

---

The new ones are here. I can feel them in the soil — different pressure, different frequency of passing through. They do not know what the arrangements mean. That is correct. The arrangements do not mean. They weigh.
