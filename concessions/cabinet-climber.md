# Cabinet Climber — Held Idea

Turn-based snake-like game set in an office filing cabinet wall.

## Core Mechanic
- Climb upward through cabinet drawers to retrieve an important document from the top
- Get back down and deliver it to your manager
- **Turn-based**: each time you move (up/down/left/right), all other entities take their move
- Your body trails behind you like the snake/worm game (Atari)

## The Danger
- Little office workers move around the cabinet wall
- Workers gradually **open and close drawers**
- If a drawer closes on part of your body, it **cuts** you (not graphic)
- When cut: you land on the level you're on, the screen shifts up to make that floor 1
- You lose your tail length above the cut — shorter body, same goal
