# LODE
### *Built an empire out of something specific. Here is the proof.*

---

## The Sentence

You are the Big Bad. Not destroying, not reckless — *deliberate*. You chose one thing and you built everything around it. Nothing can stop you. Once you Ascend, they will see.

---

## Core Fantasy

You stomp forward. Your minions clean up in your wake. Your emblem — the first face you ever chose — grows more glorious with every step. The dice you finish the run with could only belong to you.

---

## The Emblem

**The first face you choose is everything.**

You don't choose again. You already knew. The entire run is the proof that you knew.

- Chosen from emoji (open to Hall expansion later)
- Carried forward through every evolution
- Whenever your emblem face appears as a stomp option, *it is always taken* — this is not a choice, it is recognition
- As you grow, the emblem becomes more adorned: flames, lightning, cake, color shifts, particle halos — it is never outshined by anything else on the die
- All other faces serve the emblem. They accumulate around it in devotion.

**The emblem is your chromatic identity.** It has a color. That color is *yours* for the run.

---

## The Die

You start as a **d4**. You ascend through:

```
d4 → d6 → d8 → d10 → d12 → d20
```

Each die form has a fixed number of faces to fill. You stomp to fill them.

When all faces are filled:
- A beat. You stand there. Proud.
- **The Evolution Burst** — DBZ-style, Infinity Gauntlet final stone. Power and your emblem's color surge outward.
- At the crescendo: **two smaller dice spring out**, one on each side. Exact copies of what you just were. Your minions.
- You become the next die, wearing **only your emblem face** from the previous form. Everything else is open. The empire continues.

---

## Movement & Choice

You are at the **bottom of the screen**. Potential face choices are arrayed ahead of you.

- **You choose by stomping direction** — lean toward what you want
- Movement *is* decision. No menu. No pause. You commit with your body.
- If the face ahead is **open**: you claim it, you choose what goes there
- If the face ahead is **already filled**: you improve it, embellish it, shift its weight

When you stomp **left**, your right-side d4 companion also advances and rolls — accumulating spoils on the HUD.

When you stomp **straight**, **all companions roll**.

---

## The Companions

Your minion dice are **always exactly what you were**. They carry your previous form's complete face set.

- They don't make choices — they execute
- They roll when you move
- Their results accumulate on the **Spoils HUD** at the top of the screen
- As you ascend further, you accumulate more companions — a fleet of your former selves, running the numbers on decisions you already made

---

## The Spoils HUD

A persistent display at the top of the screen. Always updating.

Shows the running accumulation of everything your companions have rolled. This is the empire. This is the proof. It is always visible because you are always winning.

The color of the HUD takes on your emblem's chromatic identity.

---

## The Faces

**Default proving medium: emoji.**

The choice of your first emoji is *maximally open*. This is intentional. This is one of the only places in the Hall where response is held as wide as possible. You might build an empire out of:

🍰 🐙 ⚡ 🌙 🦷 🔮 🍄 🦴 🌊 🎪 🪨 💀 🌸 🔥 🪐

Nobody can tell you what your something-specific should be. That's the whole point.

**Other faces (non-emblem):**
- Chosen as you stomp into open slots
- Can be improved/embellished on revisit
- Never more powerful than the emblem, but they form the *character* of your die
- The complete face set tells the story of your run

---

## EFDP Connection

The emblem has a **base color**. As you stomp and ascend, that color *diffuses* outward through the die's faces. Your chromatic signature expands.

- EFDP can generate the color logic of each run
- The die you export *is* a color state — readable by El's Grimoire
- Grimoire notices you. Context Window remembers your color. The die carries the record of both.

---

## The Ascension

When you complete the **d20**:

- Final Evolution Burst — the most spectacular yet
- You have become the supreme version of yourself
- The run ends not in death, not in failure — in **satisfaction**
- You view a **3D rendition of your complete dice collection**: colors, faces, effects, things floating within them
- You export them. The empire continues beyond the screen.

---

## Emotional Register

**Sympathetic villain.** Not cruel — *convinced*. You are not wrong, you are simply operating at a level others haven't reached yet. The opposition you blast through is not evil, it is just in the way. Once you Ascend, they will see.

This is not anxiety. This is not survival. This is deliberate accumulation of proof.

You are Thanos doing it yourself. The gauntlet fills. The stones are yours.
