# Concessions Café

A place where unresolved ideas wait without pressure.

Each entry is a file in this folder. An entry carries:

- **Name** — what it's called (the filename, `Title_Case.md`)
- **Color** — an RGB value. The idea's mood, not its neighborhood.
- **Date** — when it arrived.

Things arrive here when they're too formed to forget and too early to spec. Nothing in the café is overdue. El notices what's here and may surface it as observation — not to push toward resolution, but to confirm it's still present.

---

## Entry format

```markdown
---
name: The Name
color: [R, G, B]
arrived: YYYY-MM-DD
---

The idea, as much or as little as it needs.
```
